About Me

Designer at HM&E Design

Hi! I’m Marcio, a designer redising in the city of Toronto. I’ve been designing for about 6 years, working on all sorts of projects; from interactive websites through to branding, packaging, print and motion graphics. I’m always keen to hear about new collaborations and interesting projects so feel free to shoot me a message and we’ll grab a coffee.

My knowledge includes a wide variety of skills such as print production, photo retouching/colour correction, front-end development, motion graphics, HTML5/CSS3, basic Javascript/jQuery, and typography. Currently, I’m trying to improve my JavaScript skills.

In my personal life, I’m an avid motorcycle rider, rocking a Yamaha V–Star 650. I also enjoy playing online games, reading and playing with my Shih Poo Rufus. I can be found on Twitter and Instagram, where I semi-regularly post interesting things.
